## List of Rules

## Safe Word

The safe word is **BANGARANG**

Whenever a roleplay situation becomes overwhelming or uncomfortable, the safe word can be used to immediately exit the situation. All involved parties must honor the safe word and leave the scene immediately.

<Info>
  This word is meant as an emergency situation and therefore should not be used unless absolutely necessary. All in RP scenarios should be attempted to be resolved in-character unless any party begins to feel uncomfortable or overwhelmed.
</Info>
