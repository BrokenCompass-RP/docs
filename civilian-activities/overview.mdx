This page serves as a landing page for the civilian activities section. In this section you should find all the information you need related to general activities available in Los Santos. Click each card below to get information related to the activity.

# Bahama Mamas West

Party the night away in one of Los Santos's top nightclubs. Conveniently located across the street from your apartment.

<Frame>
  ![Bahama Mama](/images/Bahama-Mama.png)
</Frame>

# Vespucci Beach Club

Join your friends at the hottest beach club in Los Santos; conveniently located between Del Perro Pier Pier and Vespucci Marketplace. This beach club offers a dance floor, multiple bars, pools, and places to lounge. Bring your friends and always be on the lookout for new faces.

<Frame>
  ![Beach Club](/images/Beach-Club.png)
</Frame>

# Bike and Skate Shop

A beach day isn't complete without a bike ride. Purchase a skateboard if that's more your style and enjoy the Vespucci skate park.

<Frame>
  ![Bike Shop](/images/Bike-Shop.png)
</Frame>

# Insert Coins Arcade

Jump in a taxi and make your way to Insert Coins Arcade. Insert Coins Arcade offers a variety of arcade games that you can play to win prizes. If you're not much of a gamer, you can always just enjoy the bar.

<Frame>
  ![Arcade](/images/Arcade.png)
</Frame>
