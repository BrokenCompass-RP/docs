## How to Get The Job

You can get the job at the City Services counter. If you don't have a vehicle you can [/taxi ](controls#taxi-service)and have it take you to the GPS marker you have. The marker is a white shield seen below.

<Frame>
  ![Job Center](/images/Job_Center.png)
</Frame>

You can always get the job back by coming here and requesting it again.

## Getting To The Job Area

You can also use [/taxi](controls#taxi-service) to take you to the job area. You'll find the job set up around Los Customs in the middle of Los Santos, north from the City Services Counter.

<Frame>
  ![Mechanic Job](/images/Mechanic_Job.png)
</Frame>

There is also a Los Customs shop set up for people to customize their vehicle there, as well as a crafting bench.

## Getting A Job Vehicle

The jobs vehicle can be obtained by standing in the 'Y' of the asphalt patch, just outside of the shop. Use your [third-eye](controls#third-eye) to interact with the garage.

<Frame>
  ![Mechanic Job Vehicle Spot](/images/Mechanic_Job_Vehicle_Spot.png)
</Frame>

## The Job Stash

The job stash is at the back of Los Customs. Target the toolbox with the [third-eye](controls#third-eye).

<Frame>
  ![Mechanic Job Stash](/images/Mechanic_Job_Stash.png)
</Frame>

## The Job Shop

You can access things you need to make repairs by using the [third-eye](controls#third-eye) to target a tool chest near the vehicle lift and blue jack.

<Frame>
  ![Mechanic Job Shop](/images/Mechanic_Job_Shop.png)
</Frame>

## The Job Sign-Off

If you want to go off duty, you can use the duty location to do so. Use your [third-eye](controls#third-eye) to interact with the door and select the option to go off duty.

<Warning>
  Going off duty will require having to select the job again from the job center.
</Warning>

<Frame>
  ![Mechanic Job Duty](/images/Mechanic_Job_Duty.png)
</Frame>
